Kitofox Remote - Privacy Policy

We do not collect any personal information.

However, if you have opted in to automatically share your Android device's usage and diagnostics data, information such as how many times the app has crashed, stack traces and other information directly related to a crash will be collected.

The Kitofox Remote Android application makes use of third party service providers that may collect information to identify you.
We strongly suggest you review the privacy policies of these service providers:

- Google Play Services - Google AdMob

This Privacy Policy may be changed in the future.
We advise you to review this page periodically for changes.
Changes become effective as soon as they are posted on this page.
