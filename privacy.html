DeskFox - Privacy Policy

We do not collect any personal information.

However, the DeskFox Android application makes use of third party service providers that collect and share information that may personally identify you.


One of these providers is Google AdMob and it collects information such as IP addresses, Approximate location, App interactions, Diagnostic information and Device and Account identifiers.
This information is collected for purposes such as Advertising and marketing, Analytics, Fraud prevention, security and compliance.


Another third party service provider through which the app collects information is Google Play Services.
Information collected goes from analytics and diagnostics to how many times the app has crashed, stack traces and other information directly related to a crash.


We strongly suggest you review the privacy policies of these service providers:

- Google Play Services - Google AdMob

Outside of the data collection from third party service providers, local network IP Addresses and passwords for the desktop client of the application are saved and processed locally.


This Privacy Policy may be changed in the future.
We advise you to review this page periodically for changes.
Changes become effective as soon as they are posted on this page.
